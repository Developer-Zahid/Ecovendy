How Serious is our plastic problem

In Singapore alone, about 930 million kg of plastic waste is discarded every year with 96% of them being non-recyclable. That means it is likely almost all of your plastic bags, bottles and containers will become plastic waste that will affect the environment even if they are dealt with properly. According to the United Nations, at least 800 species worldwide are affected by marine debris, and as much as 80 percent of that litter is plastic."

Our Ocean

Our Landfill

In Singapore, we bury our rubbish but at the rate Singaporeans are throwing out trash, Semakau is projected to last for only another 16 years! Where will all our rubbish go after 2035?

Toxic Fumes

Some countries are forced to burn their rubbish. Most of which, releases toxic fumes that could harm both us and our biodiversity
